About Us

A decade of service, solidarity, and community empowerment — built on the foundation of brotherhood.

Solapur Bagban Jamiyat (SBJ) is a registered community trust serving the Bagban community of Solapur city and district, Maharashtra. Founded on the principles of brotherhood, solidarity, and selfless service, the trust works to bridge social and economic gaps through targeted welfare programs.

From scholarships for meritorious students to emergency medical aid, from widow support to vocational training — SBJ has been a reliable pillar for thousands of families in need.

Our Mission

To provide timely, dignified support to every member of the Bagban community in need, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Vision

An empowered, educated, and self-reliant Bagban community contributing positively to Solapur and the nation.
